    2 Oto

    This is a process that making the voice marking more accurate.
    You are recommended to use SetParam(otoini tool) in this process.

  • Copy the otoini and paste it under the recording file
  • Open it by SetParam
  • Accurate the timeline of each phoneme

    • This is the sample of oto in different situations.

  • CV: Voiced Consonant

  • CV: Voiceless Consonant

  • CV: Plosive

  • CV: Affricate

  • Vv: Checked Tone

    3 Tuning

    You may confused how to use this voicebank in UTAU, let me tell you!

  • Input the cantonese CVVv dictionary in to UTAUincision(UTAU Pluguin)
  • Input the lyrics into notes with cantonese in english
  • Select them and use the Pluguin to split them into CVVv format
  • Select them again and press the space key that the voicebank sings
